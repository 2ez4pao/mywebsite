DISCOVER MY HOMETOWN

San Carlos City is always a place one likes to call home. A place that isn't perfect but brings back a bundle of memories, surrounds you with familiar crowds, those same buildings with a few more additions on every visit, the same public problems and an acquainted way of dealing with the locals. All these make us give it more importance than the rest.

SIPAWAY ISLAND more_vert
SIPAWAY close

Sipaway Island, is Geographically known as Refugio Island, a favorite destination both of local and foreign tourists. It is just 10 to 15 minutes away from San Carlos City, Negros Occidental by pumpboat. The island measures about 7 kilometers from tip-to-tip, you can cover the whole area in less than a day. The island offers white sand beaches, cliffs and clear water. Its natural resources is abundant wherein you can taste the fresh, healthy and straight from catch marine foods. The people are very friendly as well, they love tourist and they love to help in case you have inquiries.

Mayana Peak is one of the best viewpoints on the eastern coast of Negros. To reach this peak it’s just a short 15-minute hike but you’ll have to drive up into the mountains. I camped solo at the peak after riding my bicycle all the way up the steep hills from San Carlos City. In this blog post, I’ll share with you all of the details you’ll need to know to plan your visit to Mayana Peak.
